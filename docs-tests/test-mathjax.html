Tests of Conversion between Unicode and $\LaTeX$

Small greek αβγδεζηθικλμνξπρστυφχψω: $αβγδεζηθικλμνξπρστυφχψω$

Capitals greek ΓΔΘΛΞΠΣΦΨΩ: $ΓΔΘΛΞΠΣΦΨΩ$

Variants greek ςϑϒϕϖϰϵϱ: $ςϑϒϕϖϰϵϱ$

Useful symbols †‡ℓ∂∅∇∫∞: $†‡ℓ∂∅∇∫∞$

Double-struck ℂℍℕℙℚℝℤ𝔼𝕂𝕊𝕋𝕌𝟙: $ℂℍℕℙℚℝℤ𝔼𝕂𝕊𝕋𝕌𝟙$, \mathbbm{1} is not recognized by MathJax.

Quantifiers ∀∃∄: $∀∃∄$

Relations ∈∉∋∌⇐⇒⇔≤≥≪≫⩽⩾≠≈≡≢⊂⊃: $∈∉∋∌⇐⇒⇔≤≥≪≫⩽⩾≠≈≡≢⊂⊃$, \nequiv not recognized by MathJax.

Conflict with \perp for the specs of Unicode : $\LaTeX$ produces U22A5 (downtack) and not U27C2 (perpendicular). So we take U22A5 ⊥:$⊥$

Operations ∩∪∧∨×÷√⊕⊗⊛⨁⨂∘·: $∩∪∧∨×÷√⊕⊗⊛⨁⨂∘·$

Modifiers ¬±∓: $¬±∓$

Arrows ←↑→↓↦: $←↑→↓↦$

Delimiters ⟦⟧⟨⟩: $⟦⟧⟨⟩$, \llbracket and \rrbracket not recognized by MathJax.

Conflict with \Vert (or \|) for the specs of Unicode : $\LaTeX$ produces U2225 (parallel to) and not U2016 (double vertical line). So we take U2225 ∥:$∥$

Dots …⋮⋯⋰⋱: $…⋮⋯⋰⋱$, \iddots not recognized by MathJax.

Typography (french) «»: $«»$, \og and \fg{} not recognized by MathJax.
